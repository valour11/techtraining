
The internet is a global network of interconnected computers and devices that allows for the exchange of information and communication across vast distances.
It enables users to access a wide range of resources, including websites, applications, and digital content. 
The internet operates on a decentralized system of interconnected networks, facilitated by protocols such as TCP/IP, HTTP, and DNS. 
It serves as a platform for various online activities, including browsing the web, sending emails, streaming media, conducting research, and engaging in social networking. 
The internet has revolutionized how people communicate, collaborate, and access information etc. 
Almost anything can be done over the internet giving rise to diverse opportunities that wouldn't exist without it.
